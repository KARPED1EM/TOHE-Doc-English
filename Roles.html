# ⭐ Impostor

#

The bounty hunter will be randomly assigned a bounty target. If the bounty target is killed within the specified time, the kill CD will become very short, and the next kill can be performed immediately. If the bounty hunter kills someone who is not the target of the bounty, the kill CD will be extended. If the bounty hunter does not complete any kills within the specified time, the target will be randomly switched to another person.

From: TOH

#

The kill cooldown of the Serial Killer is very short, but if there is no kill within the specified time, the Serial Killer gonna suicide.

From: TOH

#

The bited target of the Vampire will not die immediately, but will die suddenly after a delay. If entering the meeting phase within this time, the target will die immediately. When killed as bait, Vampire will still be forced to report the bait's body.

From: TOH

#

The Warlock can use the kill button to cast a spell on the target, and if there is a shield-animation, the spell is successful (don't worry, only you can see it). When the Warlock shapeshift, the cursed player automatically kills the nearest player. Please note that Lazy guy will not do things for you, cursing Lazy guy will only waste your time.

From: TOH

#

The Assassin can use the kill button to mark a target, and if the mark is successful, a shield-animation will displayed as a reminder. The Assassin can then use shapeshift to teleport to the marked person and kill the marked person. Assassin cannot kills regularly, but may be able to kills regulatly after assassination according to the host's settings.

From: TOHR & Coding: KARPED1EMopen in new window

Also, hacking Lazy guy won't do things for you, hacking Lazy guy is just a waste of your time.
